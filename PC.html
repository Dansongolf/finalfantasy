Finn

Finn is a Viera who left home when he came of age. He was supposed to join the male Viera as hunters away from the village, but that wasn’t a life he wanted so he broke ‘The Green Word’ and set out to learn about the world around him, never allowed to return. He’s naive about modern city customs and traditions, and prioritizes the natural world above all else. Finn is able to communicate with elements of nature when he focuses to connect. And he recently discovered that he's sensitive to too much Aether and uncontrollably transforms.
